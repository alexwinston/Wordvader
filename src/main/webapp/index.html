Are you stuck hunting for a word in WordsWorth? This handy app will supply you with hints from a screenshot of your current game. Not sure you have the optimal combination of dots or length on your word then Wordvader is the answer. Wordvader is a perfect complement to WordsWorth for both the novice or seasoned veteran.

Features:

  • WordsWorth hints for 5x5, 6x6 and 7x7 grids.
  • WordsWorth hints grouped by dots or length.
  • WordsWorth hints for words between 4 and 8 letters.

Support:
